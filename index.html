Occupational English Test (OET) is an English proficiency test conducted for healthcare professionals intending to begin their careers abroad. The test is available for around 12 medical professions including nurses, doctors, physiotherapists, radiographers, veterinary science, dietetics, occupational therapy, optometry, podiatry, and speech pathology. OET exams are written by medical professionals for abroad studies or to restart their profession in countries like Canada, Egypt, Maldives, Malta, Namibia, Philippines, Australia, Newzealand, Singapore, Ireland, UK, etc.

Since the exams are conducted for working professionals in the healthcare sector the OET test’s governing authorities have given the freedom to choose between three types of OET test.
1. OET Test on Paper.
2. OET exam on computer at a test venue.
3. OET at Home.

On successful completion of either of the OET test, the test taker will be able to download the test report within 12 days of test completion. The complete list of OET exam dates and results will be discussed as we move ahead with the discussion.

Price of OET Exam Booking:

Healthcare professionals aiming to write OET exam should register in the portal by paying fees of $587 AUD around 33,000 INR. Registration and payment can be completed online using a valid Visa or MasterCard by either the candidates themselves or with the help of their seniors or an experienced professional in their coaching institute. OET Results Publish Time:

Candidates can access their results on the official OET website after 12 business days. The test takers can download the result in an e-format by logging in to the OET website after entering their username and password created while registering for the exam.

OET Exam Pattern:

Test takers have to attend 4 sessions for completing oet exam i.e, Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking. The pattern and time frame for which are discussed below:

Listening Reading Writing Speaking
Listening section pattern is general for all healthcare professionals. Reading section is the same for all healthcare professionals eligible to attend the OET Writing section is specific fo each healthcare professional based on their specialties. Speaking section of OET exam is also specific for each healthcare professional.
Candidates are expected to complete the test within the stipulated time of 45mins. During this time frame, the attendee has to complete 3 sections specified. Candidates should complete the exam within 60mins, they will be presented with 3 sections of 42 questions for the reading section. Usually, the test takers are asked to prepare a referral letter based on their professional situations. A speaker or an interlocutor will be assigned to asses candidates speaking ability on medical matters as in they converse with patients. It has a duration of around 20mins.
How to Check OET results:

Those candidates who have successfully attended OET exam can check their results on myoet official website

Link to my oet official website
  1. Browse with the URL and you will land on my OET official website login page.
  2. You can log in with the username and password created while registering for the OET exam.
  3. On logging in you could see the display invoice option next to the date on which you have written the test.
  4. Click on the display invoice option
  5. From the drop-down menu choose 'Download statement of result'.
